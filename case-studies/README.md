# Selected Product Case Studies

These are public, no-code copies of selected projects. They are written for recruiters, hiring managers, and collaborators who want to understand product scope, technical judgment, and business value without seeing private source code.

## Top 5

- [Serfis AI Workforce](serfis-ai-workforce/README.md) - autonomous AI agents for B2B sales and prospecting.
- [Laizy](laizy/README.md) - cloud-run job application automation with human approval gates.
- [AI Stock Analyst](ai-stock-analyst/README.md) - multi-model daily market analyst with live scoring and portfolio simulation.
- [Speak ART](speak-art/README.md) - realtime voice-to-editable-presentation builder.
- [Shopping AI](shopping-ai/README.md) - barcode-first grocery and beauty recommendation PWA.

## Selection Notes

I selected these five because they show the broadest signal across production SaaS, applied AI, automation, data products, realtime systems, payments, security-sensitive workflow design, and user-centered product thinking.

Related repositories such as Iris, Saffi Dealership, and Autopilot Resume are folded into the broader Serfis and Laizy stories where that produces a clearer recruiter narrative.
